ABOUT JAIPUR

Travel Mate

Jaipur is the capital and largest city of the state of Rajasthan in India .During British rule in India , Jaipur was the capital of the princely state of Jaipur. It has about 3 million inhabitants and was founded on November 18, 1728 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II , the ruler of Amber.It is known as "The Pink City", since in 1876 its maharaja had it painted that color, for the visit of the Prince of Wales . Since then the city is regularly painted. Jaipur is the first planned city of India, located in the semi-desert lands of Rajasthan. The city that had once been the royal capital, known as the City of the Maharajas , is now the capital of Rajasthan. The very structure of Jaipur resembles the taste of Rajputs and royal families. Today, Jaipur is an important business center with all the requirements of a metropolitan city.

Locations

AMBER FORT

Amer Fort is a fort located in Amer, Rajasthan, India. Amer is a town with an area of 4 square kilometres located 11 kilometres from Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan. Located high on a hill, it is the principal tourist attraction in Jaipur.

HAWAMAHAL

Hawa Mahal (English translation: "Palace of Winds" or "Palace of the Breeze") is a palace in Jaipur, India. It is constructed of red and pink sandstone. The palace sits on the edge of the City Palace, Jaipur, and extends to the zenana, or women's chambers.The structure was built in 1799 by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh.

CITY PALACE

City Palace, Jaipur, which includes the Chandra Mahal and Mubarak Mahal palaces and other buildings, is a palace complex in Jaipur, the capital of the Rajasthan state, India.

JANTAR MANTAR

The Jantar Mantar monument in Jaipur, Rajasthan is a collection of nineteen architectural astronomical instruments built by the Rajput king Sawai Jai Singh II, and completed in 1734.

NARGARH

Nahargarh Fort stands on the edge of the Aravalli Hills, overlooking the city of Jaipur in the Indian state of Rajasthan. Along with Amer Fort and Jaigarh Fort, Nahargarh once formed a strong defense ring for the city.

JAIGARH

Jaigarh Fort is situated on the promontory called the Cheel ka Teela of the Aravalli range; it overlooks the Amber Fort and the Maota Lake, near Amber in Jaipur, Rajasthan, India.

JALMEHAL

Jal Mahal is a palace in the middle of the Man Sagar Lake in Jaipur city, the capital of the state of Rajasthan, India. The palace and the lake around it were renovated and enlarged in the 18th century by Maharaja Jai Singh II of Amber.

ALBERT HALL

The Albert Hall Museum is a museum in Jaipur in Rajasthan, India. It is the oldest museum of the state and functions as the State museum of Rajasthan.

CHOKI DHANI

Chokhi Dhani is a concept capturing the vibrant spirit of Rajasthan and ensuring a perfect Rajasthani experience. Inbound and native tourists have a desire to experience the unique Rajasthani culture. The real Rajasthan, the villages, the paintings, Bani Thani art, the wall decorations, Dipak wall.
